SwagLabs Bug

Description

An user can go through  the checkout flow without having products added to his cart

Steps to reproduce:
1. Login to SwagLabs application
2. Click on empty cart button
3. Click on checkout button
4. Add "First Name", "Last Name" and "Zipcode" information
5. Click on continue
6. Click on finish button

Expected result:
1. An error should appear to warn the user that he has no products selected

Observed result:
1. The checkout complete page is displayed
